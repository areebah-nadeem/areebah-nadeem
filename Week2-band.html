Top three Favourite Album

Millenium

When this Album came out it CRUSHED Billboard and broke many music charts, this album came out on 2000 with 4 awesome songs "I want it that way" "larger than life" "show me the meaning" "The one"

Never Gone

I like this album because The whole band matured up and played different style of music which is not present nowadays... What makes this album so special is "INCOMPLETE" amazing single If it weren't for this album, I would've never gotten into rock/metal music. It absolutely defined my musical tastes. Not just the songs on the album, even the unreleased songs are great with great vocal ability. This is for me their best album. I hope for a new album in that style of music.

Black & Blue
